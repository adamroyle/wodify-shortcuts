Today we will spend time on both the Ring Muscle and Pistol Progressions. Even if you never want to achieve a Ring Muscle Up or Pistol it is valuable to do these drills from a neurological point of view. It is connecting your brain and body with new neurological pathways and motor recruitment patterns which is valuable for both the body and brain.
